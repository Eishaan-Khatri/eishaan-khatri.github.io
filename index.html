01 About Me

I grew up fascinated by two things — mathematics and language. I wanted to understand why equations could predict physical reality and why the same sentence in Hindi and English carried different emotional weight. That curiosity led me to Mathematics & Computing at RGIPT, where I discovered that AI sits exactly at that intersection.

Today, my work clusters around multilingual NLP for code-mixed text, robustness evaluation for vision and VLMs, and applied AI systems such as agents and RAG pipelines. I try to keep the work evidence-backed through datasets, baselines, ablations, error analysis, and reproducible artifacts.

What drives me is the intersection of rigorous mathematics and real-world impact — whether that's reducing LLM hallucinations by 68%, detecting cement plant anomalies from 378K sensor readings, or democratizing AI access for researchers with limited compute.

Currently ranked #1 in Mathematics at my university, with a Minor in Business Consulting that gives me a unique lens on translating technical depth into stakeholder value.
